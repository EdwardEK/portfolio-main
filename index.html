About A brief introduction about me.

What Do I Bring to the Table?

I work with front-end technologies and design tools while implementing a marketing approach roadmap. I'm passionate about bringing both the technical and visual aspects of digital products to life and perpetually working on improving and adding to my skills. My passion drives me to always stay up-to-date on the latest trends and utilize the newest technologies.

 

Some of my specialties include, wireframing and creating interfaces using Figma, image optimization and creating designs and logos within Adobe Creative Suite, building websites from scratch such as my portfolio website or utilizing a Content Management System (CMS), building and testing emails for marketing campaigns while executing a strategy, writing and editing content for digital products, generating responsive web designs across platforms and browsers, integrating web accessibility, Google Analytics, social media, SEO practices, and page loading speed, and maintaining websites as technology, content, and third-party services are continuously updating.

 

When I'm not coding or contemplating typefaces, I try to live an active lifestyle. Some of my interests are playing Pickleball and tennis, snow and water skiing, traveling, spending too much time scrolling through Netflix, and going on walks with Ollie, my eager to explore Husky.
